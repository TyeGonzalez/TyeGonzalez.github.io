3D Rotor Inhomogeneity Renderer (Client-side)

Pure HTML/JS version of the Dash app: computes the complex B-field on a (ρ,z,φ) grid, converts to B1+ using tilted B0 (θ=54.7°), then renders Plotly 3D scatter.
